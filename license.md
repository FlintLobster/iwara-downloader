# Iwara Video Downloader

**Key Features:**

* **Batch Downloading:** Easily download multiple videos at once.
* **Aria2 Integration:** Full support for Aria2-based downloads.
* **Link Detection:** Automatically scans video descriptions and comment sections for third-party cloud storage links.
* **Custom Saving Options:** Customize both storage paths and filenames *(Note: Custom paths are only supported for Aria2 and IwaraDownloader; other download modes only support custom filenames)*.
* **Automated Actions:** Automatically follow creators and like selected videos *(Disabled by default; can be enabled manually)*.
* **Access to Private Content:**
* Force-display unlisted and private videos *(Disabled by default; requires following the author)*.
* Download private videos *(Requires an account that is friends with the author)*.
* Download hidden videos *(Requires knowing the video ID)*.
